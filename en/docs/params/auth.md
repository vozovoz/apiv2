## Authentication

You need to pass your authentication token (auth token) in [Query string](get.md) or in [Request body](post.md),  as `token` parameter.

You can get your auth token [here](https://vozovoz.ru/dev/api/) for production purposes, and [here](https://vozovoz.org/dev/api/) for testing and developing.
